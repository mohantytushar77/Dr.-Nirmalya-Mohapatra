About the Doctor

Compassionate Care, Restoring Function

Providing tailored physical rehabilitation and state-of-the-art non-surgical pain solutions centered on getting you back to your life.

Empathetic and Comprehensive Recovery

As a specialist in Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation (PMR) and Interventional Pain Procedures, Dr. Nirmalya Mohapatra is committed to providing comprehensive, compassionate, and non-surgical care to individuals suffering from chronic pain, neuromusculoskeletal disorders, and functional limitations.

With rigorous academic training from India's premier medical institutions, including a prestigious Fellowship in Pain Medicine from AIIMS, New Delhi (completed January 2025), he combines cutting-edge clinical interventions with physical rehabilitation techniques. Whether addressing chronic spinal pain, joint disorders, complex sports-related injuries, or neurological rehabilitation, Dr. Mohapatra's patient-centered approach is focused on identifying the root cause of functional issues and designing tailored recovery paths.

Currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of PMR at Fakir Mohan Medical College & Hospital (FMMCH), Balasore, he is active in training the next generation of doctors and clinical researchers while running a practice delivering advanced medical wellness and rehabilitation services to the local community.

Medical Services

Areas of Specialization & Expertise

Combining diagnostic precision, state-of-the-art procedures, and systematic physical therapy.

Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation (PMR)

Diagnosis, evaluation, and comprehensive functional restoration for patients living with post-stroke hemiplegia, traumatic brain/spinal cord injuries, spasticity, and pediatric neuromuscular conditions.

Chronic Pain Management

Therapeutic strategies designed around non-surgical intervention for degenerative spinal disorders, chronic low back/neck pain, refractory joint pain, neuropathic pain, fibromyalgia, and complex regional pain syndromes.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Targeted kinetic rehabilitation, muscle re-education, joint biomechanical restoration, and athletic alignment protocols for knee ligament (ACL, MCL) sprains, rotator cuff strains, tennis elbow, and running injuries.

Musculoskeletal Disorders

Diagnosis and physical management of osteoarthritis, inflammatory arthropathies, frozen shoulder (adhesive capsulitis), myofascial pain syndrome, plantar fasciitis, and occupational strain syndromes.

Interventional Pain Procedures

State-of-the-art post-fellowship interventions: ultrasound-guided trigger point injections, intra-articular injections, precise fluoroscopy-guided epidural/facet blocks, and radiofrequency ablation of painful sensory nerves.

Holistic Wellness & Prevention

Long-term health guidelines focusing on posture re-education, core strengthening programs, customized occupational ergonomics, and home physical training programs to prevent pain relapse.
